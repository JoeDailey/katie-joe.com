Our love story began in Seattle in the summer of 2018 when Katie, a bright-eyed intern, joined my team. It didn’t take long to realize there was more than just workplace synergy between us. After Katie returned to college and later moved to San Francisco for work, it seemed like we might never reconnect. But in 2020, with the pandemic and remote work changing everything, Katie made the bold decision to move to Seattle to be with me. Since then, we’ve survived the pandemic, gotten a puppy, traveled to new places, and moved to Houston. Now, we’re excited to start the next chapter of our lives together!

Island Cocktail Attire

Please attend wearing cocktail attire. Bright and patterned styles are encoraged. Men should wear a suit or dress shirt. Women should wear a cocktail dress or dressy seperates. We recommend a jacket for the evenings this time of year.

Accomodations

We have 2 room blocks available for our guests. One is near the venue in beautiful sea-side La Jolla. The second is in downtown San Diego (25 minutes by car) for those who are looking to spend more time in the city proper.

The Empress

The Empress is walking distance to the venue and La Jolla's downtown and cove. Follow this link and book before April 16th for the group rate. Please note that if you want to extend your stay beyond the 16th and 17th, with the group rate, you can book two nights and then call to extend your stay.

The Westin

The Westin is in downtown San Diego, which is 25 minutes from La Jolla. There is limited parking at the venue, so please consider taking a cab to the ceremony. Follow this link and book before April 18th for the group rate.

Travel Recommendations

The closest airport is San Diego International Airport (SAN) about 14 miles from the venue. LAX is the next closest large airport, but is 2 hours away by car. Traffic between L.A. and San Diego is notoriously heavy.

For transportation during your stay, we recommend Uber/Lyft or renting a car. However, the venue has extremely limited street parking, so whichever option you choose, we recommend that you Uber/Lyft or walk to the event itself.

Registry

In place of a traditional gift registry, we would be honored to receive red envelopes. These are known as “红包“ (“hong bao”) in Chinese culture. These envelopes, which typically contain money, are a meaningful tradition that symbolize good fortune. If you still want to give a gift, we have a small registry.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my children?

Unfortunately, we can only accommodate the children of immediate family members. We hope that you will understand this decision and that you will still be able to join us on our special day.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony is at 4pm. Please give yourself time to park and find the ceremony site. Traffic can be difficult in the San Diego.

Is there Parking?

There is limited street parking at the venue. We encourage you to take a cab or walk if possible.

What will the weather be like?

The ceremony is planned for outside and dining is inside. The average temperature of La Jolla in May is 70°F, but the temperature drops after sunset.
