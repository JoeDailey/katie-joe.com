Island Cocktail Attire

Please attend wearing cocktail attire. Bright and patterned styles are encoraged. Men should wear a suit or dress shirt. Women should wear a cocktail dress or dressy seperates. We recommend a jacket for the evenings this time of year.

Accomodations

We have 2 room blocks available for our guests. One is near the venue in beautiful sea-side La Jolla. The second is in downtown San Diego (25 minutes by car) for those who are looking to spend more time in the city proper.

Travel Recommendations

The closest airport is San Diego International Airport (SAN) about 14 miles from the venue. LAX is the next closest large airport, but is 2 hours away by car. Traffic between L.A. and San Diego is notoriously heavy.

For transportation during your stay, we recommend Uber/Lyft or renting a car. However, the venue has extremely limited street parking, so whichever option you choose, we recommend that you Uber/Lyft or walk to the event itself.

Registry

In place of a traditional gift registry, we would be honored to receive red envelopes. These are known as “红包“ (“hong bao”) in Chinese culture. These envelopes, which typically contain money, are a meaningful tradition that symbolize good fortune. If you don't want to bring cash with you or still want to give a gift, we have Venmo.

Things to Do

La Jolla Cove: Watch hundreds of sea lions and seals in their natural habitat up and down the shoreline. For the more adventurous, there are kayaking tours offered around the cove.
Torrey Pines State Natural Reserve: Hiking area with lots of trails of varying difficulty. You'll hike through some cliffside mountains and down to the beach.
San Diego Zoo: One of the best zoos and the most visited zoo in the country.
Balboa Park: A large park with interesting architecture, museums, and gardens. The zoo is also inside this park.
San Diego Zoo Safari Park: The sister location to the San Diego Zoo. Get up close and personal with the animals on a tram ride through the enclosures.
USS Midway: A historical naval aircraft carrier museum.
SeaWorld: The famous marine theme park.
Legoland: Theme park for lovers of all things Lego. This is in Carlsbad, which is about 26 miles from La Jolla.

The ceremony is at 4pm. Please give yourself time to park and find the ceremony site. Traffic can be difficult in the San Diego.

There is limited street parking at the venue. We encourage you to take a cab or walk if possible.

What will the weather be like?

The ceremony is planned for outside and dining is inside. The average temperature of La Jolla in May is 70°F, but the temperature drops after sunset.
